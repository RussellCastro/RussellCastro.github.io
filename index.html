01. About

I'm an undergraduate student at UC Berkeley studying Applied Mathematics and Computer Science. My work spans from embedded firmware in C/C++ to GPU-accelerated scientific computing and ML research.

Currently an Embedded Software Intern at Formlabs, where I ship production firmware and cloud integrations for 3D printer product lines, and a Student Researcher at BeBOP (Berkeley Benchmarking and OPtimization), working on GPU-efficient neural networks and sparse matrix methods.

Previously at Lawrence Berkeley National Lab (NERSC), I worked on machine-learning interatomic potentials, molecular dynamics on HPC systems, and quantum computing algorithms.
